Some History

How it started...

Established in 1812 and named after the town’s best fish "cacciatore nel Mundo", Tony Carlucci, our lobster-home is a family friendly venue delighting customers again and again with its home style breakfast and lunch (dinner is served Friday and Saturday nights). Seafood lovers know to head to Carlucci's® Fish House for some of the freshest seafood dishes available. Next door to Bell Buoy, their seafood specialty store, this family-owned and operated café is a casual joint where guests can savor some of the tastiest clam chowder around. The menu also features great options such as fish and chips made with halibut, wild salmon, or cod, seaside razor clams either grilled or deep fried, and crab cakes.
